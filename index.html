🧠 Strategist's Analysis — Why the metrics shifted
Why Avg Position moved from 15.8 → 20.4: By targeting high-volume terms like "electrician near me" we entered SERPs at positions 15–30 initially. This mathematically lowers the average while doubling the brand's total visibility footprint.
Why CTR shifted from 2% → 1.1%: Moving from branded to discovery traffic is expected. 1.1% of 171K = 1,880 clicks vs 2% of 83K = 1,660 clicks. We're getting more clicks in absolute terms while reaching entirely new audiences.
